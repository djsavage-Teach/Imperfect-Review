Structural foundations for a vivid narrative.

When to use the Imperfect

  • Habitual Actions: Things you "used to do" repeatedly (The video vs the photo).
  • Atmosphere: Setting the scene (Weather, time, physical surroundings).
  • Internal States: Descriptions of age, emotions, and feelings.
  • Background Narrative: The continuous action interrupted by specific events.

Conjugation Paradigm

Subject -AR Verbs (-aba) -ER / -IR Verbs (-ía)
Yo / Él / Ella jugaba comía
Nosotros jugábamos comíamos
Ellos / Ustedes jugaban comían

*Note: Accents are non-negotiable for precision.

The Correction Lab

Analyzing real-world drafts to elevate our prose.

"Ahora yo no Tengo mucho tiempo y yo no jugar en El parque."
Linguistic Cue
An infinitive is static, like a dictionary entry. Give this childhood habit a soul by matching it to the ongoing past tense.
"En 2016 El invierno estuve muy frio."
Linguistic Cue
Was the cold winter a single 'event' that finished in a second, or was it the background setting of your whole environment?
"Cuandó era jovenes, mi viva era muy divertido."
Linguistic Cue
Check the "Who." If you are talking about yourself, should the adjective be plural? If you mean "we," the verb must follow.
"La foto es en 2013 y la día son el diecisies de septembre."
Linguistic Cue
Is 'día' truly feminine? Also, if the photo is from 2013, should the description be in the present or the past?
"Yo comaba El helado con mi padre frequalmente."
Linguistic Cue
The phonetic lure of "-aba" is strong, but look at the infinitive: 'Comer.' Does an -ER verb belong to the "-aba" or "-ía" family?
"Cuando yo era una niña yo estaba muy feliz."
Linguistic Cue
Being happy as a child describes a characteristic state over time. Which verb, 'Ser' or 'Estar,' defines your essence in the past?
"La nina en la foto quieraba iba al parque."
Linguistic Cue
The Imperfect is wonderfully regular. Does the 'ie' change from the present tense (quiero) actually survive in the past?
"El clema estaba muy sol."
Linguistic Cue
The climate doesn't physically *become* the sun. Think about the verb we use to describe what the weather "makes" or "does."
"requerdo los tiempos cuando estuve jovenes."
Linguistic Cue
Foundation blocks: Use the Imperfect for ongoing states like 'being young,' and ensure your spelling of 'recuerdo' is accurate!
"Cuando yo era jovenes, mi viva era muy divertido."
Linguistic Cue
Are we using the action 'I live' or the noun 'my life'? In Spanish, ensure you use 'vida' to anchor your description.
